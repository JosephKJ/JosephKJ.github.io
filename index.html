PhD Student, Computer Science Department, Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad

I am a PhD student at IIT Hyderabad. I am fortunate to have Dr. Vineeth N Balasubramanian, as my advisor. I work on building machine learning models that can learn incrementally, just like humans do. I am also interested in generative modelling, learning under weak supervision and vision problems like detection and segmentation.

Before joining IIT Hyderabad, I was working at Oracle, as a Senior Software Engineer for 3.5 years. I used to develop softwares for PeopleSoft PeopleTools Platforms team.
